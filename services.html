WHAT WE DO

Our Services

Full-service home improvement for residential and commercial properties — from flooring and concrete coatings to roofing, remodeling, landscaping, and more.

CONCRETE COATINGS & REPAIR

Concrete Coatings & Repair

We transform bare or damaged concrete into clean, finished surfaces for garages, basements, driveways, and commercial floors. Every job starts with proper surface prep.

Garage Floor Coating

Epoxy and polyaspartic systems for single-car to multi-car garages. Protects against oil, stains, and daily wear with a professional, seamless finish.

Basement & Commercial Coatings

Heavy-duty coating systems for basements, warehouses, retail spaces, and commercial facilities built for high-traffic environments.

Concrete Repair & Leveling

Crack filling, spalling repair, and surface leveling to restore structural integrity and prepare surfaces for any new coating or flooring system.

FLOORING INSTALLATION

Flooring Installation for Every Space

From kitchens and living rooms to bedrooms and commercial spaces, we install quality flooring that looks great and holds up over time.

Hardwood Flooring

Solid and engineered hardwood installation for living areas, bedrooms, and hallways. We also sand and refinish existing hardwood to restore its original appearance.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

Waterproof and durable — works in any room including kitchens, bathrooms, and basements. Looks like hardwood, performs better in wet or high-traffic areas.

Tile & Carpet Installation

Porcelain, ceramic, and stone tile for floors and wet areas, plus carpet installation for bedrooms, offices, and commercial spaces.

REMODELING

Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling

Full renovation services for kitchens and bathrooms — from tile and fixtures to complete gut remodels. We handle every phase of the project.

Kitchen Remodeling

Cabinet updates, countertop installation, flooring, backsplash, and layout reconfiguration. Partial refreshes or full gut remodels handled start to finish.

Bathroom Remodeling

Shower and tub surrounds, vanity installs, floor tile, fixtures, and waterproofing. We turn outdated bathrooms into clean, functional spaces.

Tile & Backsplash Work

Precision tile installation for kitchen backsplashes, shower surrounds, feature walls, and bathroom floors using porcelain, ceramic, and natural stone.

INTERIOR FINISHING

Painting, Drywall & Interior Finishing

Interior finishing services that complete any renovation — from fresh paint and drywall repair to trim, baseboards, and all finish carpentry.

Interior Painting

Professional interior painting for walls, ceilings, trim, and doors. Proper surface prep, clean lines, and quality finishes for residential and commercial spaces.

Drywall Repair & Installation

Patch work, full drywall installation, texturing, and smooth finishing for new construction, remodels, and damage repair throughout the home.

Trim, Baseboard & Finish Work

Crown molding, baseboard, door casing, and all interior trim installation. The finish work that makes a renovation look polished and complete.

ROOFING & EXTERIOR

Roofing, Gutters & Siding

Exterior protection services for residential and light-commercial properties — roofing, drainage systems, and siding that keep the structure sound and the curb appeal strong.

Roofing Installation & Repair

Asphalt shingle, flat roof, and metal roofing installation and repair. We assess existing roofs and provide transparent written quotes before any work begins.

Gutter Installation & Cleaning

Seamless gutter installation, downspout routing, gutter guard systems, and seasonal cleaning to protect your foundation and landscaping from water damage.

Siding & Exterior Cladding

Vinyl, fiber cement, and wood siding installation and replacement. Improves insulation, weather resistance, and exterior appearance for any property type.

WINDOWS & DOORS

Windows, Doors & Exterior

Window and door replacement services that improve energy efficiency, security, and curb appeal — with clean, professional installation every time.

Window Replacement

Single-hung, double-hung, casement, and sliding window replacement. Energy-efficient options available to reduce heating and cooling costs year-round.

Door Installation

Entry doors, interior doors, patio doors, and sliding glass doors. We handle framing, installation, weatherstripping, and hardware for a complete, secure fit.

Exterior Renovation

Fascia, soffit, trim, and exterior finish work that ties together the look of your home's exterior and protects it from moisture and weather damage.

OUTDOOR LIVING

Decks, Patios & Outdoor Living

Custom outdoor construction that extends your living space — decks, patio surfaces, and outdoor structures built to last through every season.

Deck Construction

Custom wood and composite deck builds for residential properties. We handle design, permitting coordination, framing, decking, and railings.

Patio Coatings & Resurfacing

Epoxy and decorative coatings for concrete patios, pool decks, and driveways. Slip-resistant, weather-resistant finishes that transform outdoor surfaces.

Outdoor Living Spaces

Pergolas, covered patios, outdoor kitchens, and shade structures that extend your usable living area and add lasting value to any residential property.

LANDSCAPING & FENCING

Landscaping, Hardscaping & Fencing

Complete exterior property services — from lawn and landscaping to stone work, retaining walls, and fencing that define and protect the property.

Landscaping & Lawn Care

Landscape design, grading, sod installation, planting, and ongoing maintenance for residential and commercial properties.

Hardscaping & Pavers

Natural stone, concrete paver, and brick installations for walkways, driveways, patios, and retaining walls. Built to hold up under weather and foot traffic.

Fencing Installation

Wood, vinyl, chain-link, and ornamental fencing for residential and commercial properties. Privacy, security, and decorative options installed to local code.
