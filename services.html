WHAT WE DO

Our Services

Professional concrete coatings and flooring installation for residential and commercial properties across our four service areas.

CONCRETE COATINGS

Epoxy & Polyaspartic Floor Systems

High-performance coating systems that protect and transform concrete surfaces for garages, basements, commercial floors, and more.

Garage Floor Epoxy

Single-car to multi-car garage systems including diamond grinding, crack repair, base coat, decorative flake broadcast, and topcoat. Resists oil, chemicals, and heavy vehicle traffic.

Polyaspartic Coatings

Fast-curing, UV-stable polyaspartic systems that can be installed in one day and handle foot traffic within hours. Excellent for both residential and commercial applications.

Decorative Flake Systems

Full-broadcast or partial-flake systems in a wide range of color blends. Provides a professional showroom finish with excellent slip resistance and easy maintenance.

Basement Floor Coatings

Moisture-tolerant epoxy systems designed for below-grade slabs. Seals the concrete, resists moisture vapor, and creates a finished, cleanable surface for your basement.

Commercial Floor Coatings

Industrial-grade epoxy and urethane systems for warehouses, retail floors, medical facilities, restaurants, and other commercial spaces requiring durable, compliant flooring.

Concrete Repair & Prep

Professional crack filling, spalling repair, and diamond grinding surface preparation. A proper substrate is the foundation of a long-lasting coating — we never skip this step.

FLOORING INSTALLATION

Premium Flooring for Every Room

We install a full range of flooring materials for kitchens, living areas, bedrooms, bathrooms, and commercial spaces.

Hardwood Flooring

Solid and engineered hardwood installation including nail-down, glue-down, and floating methods. We also offer sanding and refinishing of existing hardwood floors.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

Waterproof, scratch-resistant LVP that looks like hardwood but performs in kitchens, bathrooms, and basements where real wood is not suitable.

Tile Installation

Porcelain, ceramic, and natural stone tile installation for floors, showers, backsplashes, and entryways. Includes proper substrate prep and waterproofing.
