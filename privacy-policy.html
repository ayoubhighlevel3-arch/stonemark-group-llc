Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026  |  Stonemark Group LLC

Stonemark Group LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website stonemarkgroupllc.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our Site or contact us about our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Property address or ZIP code (for scheduling estimates)
  • Service type and project details
  • Any other information you include in contact or estimate request forms

We may also automatically collect certain technical data when you visit our Site, such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and referring URL, through standard web server logs or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide estimate appointments
  • Schedule and perform concrete coating and flooring services
  • Send appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups
  • Send SMS/text messages if you have consented to receive them
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. SMS / Text Message Communications

If you opt in to SMS communications by checking the SMS consent box on our forms, you consent to receive text messages from Stonemark Group LLC at the phone number you provided. These messages may include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Estimate follow-ups
  • Service updates related to your project

Message frequency may vary (up to 5 messages per month). Message and data rates may apply. To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any text message. Reply HELP for help. Opting out of SMS does not affect your ability to receive services.

Mobile opt-in data and consent will not be shared with, sold to, or used by third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our website or delivering services (e.g., form processing, email delivery, scheduling tools), under confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law, regulation, or court order
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Stonemark Group LLC, our customers, or others

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the information below.

6. Cookies

Our Site may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience. For more information, see our Cookie Policy.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our Site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
