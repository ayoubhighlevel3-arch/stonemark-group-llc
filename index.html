What we do

Stonemark Group is a full-service home improvement group. From flooring and concrete coatings to roofing, remodeling, and outdoor construction — we handle it all with professional-grade crews.

Concrete Coatings & Repair

Epoxy and polyaspartic coating systems for garages, basements, and commercial slabs. Includes crack repair, leveling, and full surface preparation.

Flooring Installation

Hardwood, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), tile, and carpet installation for kitchens, living rooms, bedrooms, and commercial spaces.

Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling

Full kitchen and bathroom renovations including tile work, fixture updates, backsplash installation, and complete gut remodels.

Painting, Drywall & Interior Finishing

Interior painting, drywall repair and installation, trim, baseboard, and all finish work to complete any renovation project.

Roofing, Gutters & Siding

Roof installation and repair, gutter systems, and exterior siding for residential and light-commercial properties.

Windows, Doors & Exterior

Window replacement, door installation, and exterior renovation work to improve energy efficiency, curb appeal, and security.

Decks, Patios & Outdoor Living

Custom deck construction, patio coatings and resurfacing, and outdoor living space builds for residential properties.

Landscaping, Hardscaping & Fencing

Landscaping, stone and paver work, retaining walls, and fencing installation to complete the exterior of any property.
