What we install

From garage epoxy to full-home flooring, Stonemark Group handles every project with professional-grade materials and experienced crews.

Concrete Coatings

Epoxy and polyaspartic coating systems for garage floors, basements, driveways, and commercial slabs. Durable, seamless, and easy to clean.

Flooring Installation

Hardwood, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), tile, and carpet installation for kitchens, living rooms, bedrooms, and commercial spaces.

Concrete Repair & Prep

Crack filling, spalling repair, and professional surface preparation to ensure your new coating or flooring bonds correctly and lasts for years.
