STONEMARK GROUP LLC

A group built on craft, scale, and accountability.

Stonemark Group LLC is a home improvement group providing a full range of residential and commercial services — from flooring and concrete coatings to roofing, remodeling, landscaping, and more.

Registered as a limited liability company in the United States, Stonemark Group LLC was built to be a single point of contact for the full range of home improvement needs — a contractor group that operates at scale without sacrificing quality or communication. The company's registered administrative address is in Wyoming; all field operations are managed locally from within each service market.

We operate regional crews across our service markets, covering everything from flooring and concrete coatings to roofing, kitchen and bathroom remodeling, painting, windows and doors, decks, landscaping, hardscaping, and fencing. Each market is managed by a dedicated operations coordinator who oversees scheduling, crew quality, and customer satisfaction.

Stonemark Group serves homeowners, real estate investors, property managers, HOAs, and commercial property owners. Whether it's a single garage floor coating or a full property renovation across multiple units, we bring the same professional standards to every project.

Our Leadership
