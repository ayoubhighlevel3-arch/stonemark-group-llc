OUR STORY

Built on quality. Backed by craftsmanship.

Stonemark Group LLC is a home improvement company focused on delivering long-lasting results for homeowners and businesses.

Stonemark Group LLC is a home improvement company registered in the United States. We specialize in concrete coating systems and flooring installation for residential and commercial properties across our four service areas: Seattle, Nashville, Charlotte, and Scottsdale.

Our services include epoxy floor coatings, polyaspartic systems, decorative flake finishes, hardwood and LVP flooring installation, tile work, and concrete surface repair. Every project starts with a free, no-obligation estimate and is backed by a 5-year workmanship warranty.

We work with homeowners, property managers, and commercial clients. Our approach is straightforward: show up on time, use professional-grade materials, do the job right, and stand behind our work.
